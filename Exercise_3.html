EXERCISE 3


Exercise steps

  • To start, create sketch on XY plane and create a rectangular from origin with dimensions of length 40 and height 30
  • Next, create a circle using the midpoint of retangle to corner(circle with two point could work also) with a diameter of 40
  • Next, extrude the model with distance of 10
  • After done, create a rectangular sketch on the extruded model, constraint with a width of 10 and height 40
  • Next, extude the rectangular sketch with distance of 30
  • After that, create a rectangular sketch on the extruded rectangle, with dimension of width 30 and height 40
  • Extrude the sketch by distance 10
  • Lastly, fillet designated corners with radius 10 and create the holes feature with diameter 10
