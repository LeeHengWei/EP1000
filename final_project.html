User manual
Sensors are the switch to turn on or off to detect someone or object, there are 3 modes for the lamp (1. Rainbow Color, 2.Warm Color, 3.Cool Color)
When sensor detect, press the button to activate the lamp in different mode.
When sensor detect nothing within certain range for some time, it will auto turn off
