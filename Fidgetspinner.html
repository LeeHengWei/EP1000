FIDGET SPINNER


Design steps

  • Draw a construction circle with diameter 90mm , coincide the midpoint to origin, which this can be use as size guideline.
  • Start to design own fidget spinner, one mentioned here is one of the design
  • For hole size, give a tolerance of 0.1 ~ 0.2 mm (exp: bearing is diameter 13mm, hole size about 13.1 or 13.2mm)
  • Next, Extrude the model to a height, max is 8
  • Lastly, fillet or chamfer the edges to make the design looks better(appearance change if want to)
